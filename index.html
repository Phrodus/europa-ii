About

Europa II was a Minecraft Server with Towny. I created it in 2020 so that I coud play with friends after they were banned from another server that we played on, Earth but better. We originally tested custom maps, but opted for a vanilla seed. The server grew, and outlived Earth but better. However, in the months leading up to today, people stopped playing on the server. I have decided to close the server, and would like to thank all those who played on it. - Phrodus
